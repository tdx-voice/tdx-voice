About

TDX Voice provides managed VoIP and business communications services to small business clients. SMS messaging is used exclusively for operational communication with existing clients and vendors, and to receive one-time passcodes and MFA verification codes from third-party business services.

Who We Message

TDX Voice sends and receives SMS only with individuals who have an established business relationship with TDX Voice and have provided a mobile number through onboarding or ongoing account management.

Message Types

Messages are conversational and operational in nature. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on business need.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to opt out of non-operational SMS communication.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance. You may also contact us at info@tdxvoice.com. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
