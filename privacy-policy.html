Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Overview

TDX Voice ("we," "our," or "us") is a managed VoIP and business communications provider serving small businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services, including any SMS communications.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and manage our services, including business contact information (name, phone number, email address) provided during onboarding or account management, and records of communications necessary for service delivery and support.

We do not collect sensitive personal information and we do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Communications

Phone numbers collected for SMS communication are used solely for operational and conversational messaging between TDX Voice and its business clients and vendors. This includes service-related notifications and the receipt of one-time passcodes or MFA verification codes from third-party services.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information shared with SMS providers (carriers and aggregators) is limited to what is required for message delivery and compliance.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to deliver and support our managed VoIP services, communicate with clients and vendors regarding account and service matters, and comply with applicable laws and carrier requirements.

Data Retention

We retain contact information for the duration of the business relationship and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for legal or operational purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our services rely on third-party providers including VoIP carriers and SMS aggregators. These providers have their own privacy practices and we encourage you to review them as applicable.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@tdxvoice.com.
